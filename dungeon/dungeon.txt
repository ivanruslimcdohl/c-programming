Infinite Tomb Raider
- Guide your character ('Y') using W, A, S, D key to explore the dungeon
- You can not pass through wall ('#')
- Collect the infinite gold ('$')
- Your character staring point will be randomized within allowed location
- Gold will be randomly generated within allowed location after the previous one is collected
- Every movement (valid or not) is counted toward your move count
- Calculate the score

Have fun!
